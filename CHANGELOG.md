- 🎉 First release of **Lottie Studio**

- Play / pause / restart controls
- Loop toggle
- Speed slider
- Zoom in/out & fit
- Background color & theme toggle
- FPS counter
- Scrub timeline & drag to seek
- Export as `.json` and `.lottie` zip
- Frame preview thumbnails on hover
